## What is QuietMic?

QuietMic is a tiny macOS utility that lives in your menu bar and monitors your microphone input level in real time. When your voice gets too loud during meetings, it gives you a gentle visual alert — before your family, coworkers or roommates have to say anything.

It is not a professional decibel meter. It is a practical voice awareness tool for people who speak too loudly on calls without realizing it.

## Who is it for?

- Remote workers who get complaints from family members during calls
- Professionals in open offices who wear headphones and lose track of their volume
- Sales reps, managers and consultants who get louder when they are focused or excited
- Anyone who wants a private, discreet reminder to lower their voice during video calls

## How it works

1. Install QuietMic on your Mac — it lives quietly in the menu bar
2. Choose your microphone and set your volume threshold
3. Get a gentle visual alert when your voice crosses your chosen limit

Works with Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, Slack, Loom and any app that uses your Mac's microphone.

## Privacy

QuietMic monitors volume level only — not what you say.

- Runs 100% locally on your Mac
- No audio recording, no transcription, no speech analysis
- No data sent to any server
- Audio never leaves your Mac
